Data Visualization

Thesis Projects and Research
2016

The interdisciplinary Data Visualization graduate program brings together design, statistics, and computer science, developing critical abilities and facility for innovation in work settings in which data increasingly shapes opinion, policy, and decision making in all sectors. The following projects and research focus on wearable technology, the leadership gender gap, computation at the planetary scale, genetic diversity of plant species, the topology of Dante, food security in Canada's north, and the prevention of child maltreatment.
